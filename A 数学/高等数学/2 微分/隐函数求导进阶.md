隐函数求导进阶 #flashcard 

$$F(x,y,z)=a$$
此时方程右边为一个常数，求导后依旧为0，不改变原求导结果
$$F(x,y,z)=z$$
$$\frac{\partial F}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial F}{\partial z}\times\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}=\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}$$
$\therefore$ 可以通过移项得到
$$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac{\frac{\partial F}{\partial x}}{1-\frac{\partial F}{\partial z}}$$
